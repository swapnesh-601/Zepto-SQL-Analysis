## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Zepto's product inventory dataset using SQL. The objective is to uncover valuable business insights related to pricing, discounts, inventory management, category performance, and revenue opportunities.

The project demonstrates practical SQL skills including filtering, aggregation, grouping, sorting, conditional logic, and business-oriented analytical queries commonly used by Data Analysts and Business Analysts.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Zepto is one of India's fastest-growing quick-commerce platforms, offering grocery and daily essentials delivery.

The goal of this project is to analyze product-level data and answer important business questions regarding:

- Product pricing strategy
- Discount effectiveness
- Inventory management
- Revenue estimation
- Category-wise performance
- Product value comparison

---

## 📊 Business Questions Solved

### Q1. Top 10 Best-Value Products

Find the top 10 products offering the highest discount percentage.

### Q2. High MRP Products Out of Stock

Identify products with high MRP values that are currently unavailable.

### Q3. Category-wise Revenue Estimation

Calculate estimated revenue generated by each product category.

### Q4. Premium Products with Low Discounts

Find products where:

- MRP > ₹500
- Discount < 10%

### Q5. Categories with Highest Average Discounts

Identify the top 5 categories providing the highest average discount percentage.

### Q6. Best Value by Price per Gram

Calculate price per gram for products above 100g and rank products by value.

### Q7. Product Segmentation

Group products into inventory segments:

- Low
- Medium
- Bulk

based on weight and quantity characteristics.

### Q8. Inventory Weight Analysis

Calculate the total inventory weight available for each category.
